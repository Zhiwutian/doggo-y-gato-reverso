Welcome to Doggo Y Gato Reverso!

The object is to flip as many tiles to your chosen furry friend as possible!

Player 1 is Doggo. Player 2 is Gato.

Flip tiles by bracketing your opponent's little buddy with your own!

Moves are only possible if they result in a flip and are indicated with a purple square.

The player with the highest amount of fur babies on the board when there are are no more moves, or the board is full, wins!
